I'll get back to you on that. In the meantime, here are some of my recent obesssions.

* upcycled technical fashion *

The creator in me saw a discarded promotional banner destined for the landfill and didn't think it was trash, but rather an opportunity to create a whole collection of technical bags and gear. I saw the potential in this tough, water-resistant, canvas-like texture and began to produce tote bags and wallets.

I soon leveled up and began to design technical gear for outdoor adventures. I designed and sewed an insert to organize and protect climbing gear, and a foldable, origami-inspired wetsuit bag.

* tic tac toe *

I love creating hands-on, tactile objects that spark childlike joy in older users. My imagination has been captured by the simple yet elegant game of tic tac toe.

I first used JavaScript to recreate the classic game, which you can play here

Now, I am developing a giant electronic version using an abandoned LED matrix found at the maker space where I work. This has been an exploration teaching myself Arduino and basic electronics.

* hacker jewelry *

One of my favorite discoveries at the intersection of art and technology: beautiful wearables produced with engineering-oriented tools. This is what you get when you give an artist a laser cutter and a 3D printer.
