* friendship park: an urban-ecological study *

Skills: Urban Design, Conceptual Architectural Visualization

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, Inkscape

What does a border wall mean to the ocean? What does it mean to a bird, an insect, or a bear? The U.S.-Mexico border region between San Diego and Tijuana at Friendship Park is but a pixel in the mosaic of the borderlands, but one which stands out in its urban and environmental contradictions.



In February 2024, I hosted a printmaking workshop at the EnVision Arts and Engineering Maker Studio where I work. Over 20 people came and learned to linocut, making their own Valentine's Day cards for their loved ones.

I love bringing more creatives and artists into this traditionally engineering-oriented space, or introducing craft to otherwise STEM-oriented students.
