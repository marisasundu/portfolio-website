* virtual classroom with qualcomm *

Skills: Unreal Engine | Revit | VR Development | BIM Model Manipulation

As an intern at Qualcomm Institute's Center for Cyber-Archeology and Sustainability, I worked to develop an interactive Virtual/Augmented Reality (VR/AR) classroom experience.

We were tasked with designing a digital twin of a UC San Diego classroom so students physically present could enter the Metaverse with students not present and interact with each other in real time.



- process -

Our first steps involved observing products currently available to VR users for metaverse professional collaboration.

From these insights, we were able to identify the best features available which we would include in our design.

I was responsible for refining the original Revit model to match the built environment. We aligned a point cloud scan to our Unreal model, allowing us to reference the real classroom as we designed the virtual one.

I also added critical details to the model with Unreal Engine; adding custom textures and importing BIM models of furniture and other classroom accessories into the virtual environment.
